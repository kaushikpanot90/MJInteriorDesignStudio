Vibrant Habitat

Groundedness of raw concrete along with an explosion of vibrant colours.

If Vibrant colours and dynamism is your thing, then why settle for a monotonous layout?. A perfect balance of beautiful vibrant colours that are pleasing to the eye and a modernistic perspective makes the space a delight to be in. The meticulous design of the carpet along with the minimalistic wooden semicircle table gives the feel of a mix of geometric minimalism and contemporary, artistic masterpiece. The circular wall art is an aesthetic vibrant colour bomb which is contrasted with raw concrete grey walls.

The colour contrast is done very meticulously which is evident in the vibrant cushion colours on the grey sofas and the neat arrangement of the beautifully designed colourful sofa between them. Everything from the curtains and their colours, to the arrangement and positioning of lights, has been designed to match the overarching theme of the space. Wall masterpieces like the seated buddha with the beautiful design painted entirely white against the grey background and the brick mortar white washed design in the wall in the bedroom create a feeling of rawness and unfiltered primitiveness. This is well balanced with the vibrant coloured walls with geometric shapes and intricate design. The groundedness of raw concrete along with an explosion of dynamic colours makes the space a true epitome of brave colour explorations with a balanced design.
