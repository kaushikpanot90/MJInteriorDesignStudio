Accent Home

The perfect dramatic wonder, but – with effortlessness!

This project was well-intended and thoughtfully designed to create a theme of the space that could give the feeling of “ease” seasoned with a bit of dramatic wonder. The space appears clean and basic, with the light brown monochromatic sofas and white round table at the centre. The pillow colours and wall centrepiece do a great job of adding the needed contrast to give the space aliveness and freshness. The flavour of ease that design gives, is amplified by the simple engravings on the blissful snow-white walls, and the royal-looking greyish white curtains.

But, this only accounts for a piece of the cake! The gold makes it all rich, pure, abundant and wonderfully dramatic. The golden wall towards the left of the entrance does exactly that – gives the needed dramatic amazement, almost to the degree of an unexpected revelation. The wooden partition with geometric simplicity and chess-like arrangement of it with the semi-transparent glass, adds to the uniqueness of the space. Overall the design is such that one can probably call it “the perfect dramatic-ness but – with effortlessness!”
