The Evergreen Home

The perfect blend of classic ancient and modern lavishness.

Time travel isnâ€™t possible, or maybe a very distant dream. But imagine bringing the classics, the ancient immaculacy and the modern abundance and even futuristic sophistication to the present. What would it feel like? The evergreen home is designed to give the wonder and awe of time travel to the pure ancient and modern renewal while sipping your favourite cup of coffee, and relaxing on the sofa. As soon as you enter the wooden wall piece engraved with Sanskrit mantras catches your attention. The rich blue coloured sofas with the contrasting greyish white pillows are meant to make one feel the lavishness of relaxation. The wall clock and the black and white portrait sketch inside a glass box with wooden frames, beneath it, give a perfect historic feel. Towards the front, the shiny greyish background against the television is contrasted by the heavy wooden anciently designed table in front of it. The intricate design of the white curtains against the blue sofas and the overall arrangement of lights add to the rich fancy look and is in perfect contrast with the overall theme of the space. Unique in spirit and amazingly different in design, this project of ours is one of our favourite masterpieces.
