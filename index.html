About us

MJ interior design studio is a full-service interior design firm based in Ahmedabad. We provide custom interior design services and creative solutions for residential as well as commercial projects.

The studio was founded in 2020, by Maulik Jepiwala, who graduated from the prestige CEPT university with a degree in interior design. Passionate about his craft, he has built his professional career in interior design from scratch, from just being a recent graduate to owning the experience of various unique residential projects in his credit, which later evolved to demonstrating excellence in various medium & large scale projects.

Vibrant Habitat
Groundedness of raw concrete along with an explosion of vibrant colours.

If Vibrant colours and dynamism is your thing, then why settle for a monotonous layout?. A perfect balance of beautiful vibrant colours that are pleasing to the eye and a modernistic perspective makes the space a delight to be in. The meticulous design of the carpet along with the minimalistic wooden semicircle table gives the feel of a mix of geometric minimalism and contemporary, artistic masterpiece. The circular wall art is an aesthetic vibrant colour bomb which is contrasted with raw concrete grey walls. The colour contrast is done very meticulously which is evident in the vibrant cushion colours on the grey sofas and the neat arrangement of the beautifully designed colourful sofa between them. Everything from the curtains and their colours, to the arrangement and positioning of lights, has been designed to match the overarching theme of the space. Wall masterpieces like the seated buddha with the beautiful design painted entirely white against the grey background and the brick mortar white washed design in the wall in the bedroom create a feeling of rawness and unfiltered primitiveness. This is well balanced with the vibrant coloured walls with geometric shapes and intricate design. The groundedness of raw concrete along with an explosion of dynamic colours makes the space a true epitome of brave colour explorations with a balanced design.

The Rathore’s Abode
Simple yet exotic!

The clarity and simplicity of minimalism were to be reflected in this project. The hazel blue minimalist sofas contrasted with the rough, organic brown furniture give the hall a straightforward, elementary yet leading-edge look. The design reflects simplicity but not a single detail is left out. From the antique block-grey ceiling to the complementary design of the floor tiles and ceramic television background, the details never lose sight. From the colour and size of the flower vase to the placement of the lights and royal yet simple curtains, the space cannot help but give an exotic experience. And one cannot just miss the silver antelope masterpiece beneath the television which sits perfectly against the simplistic well-crafted lined brown wall. Right from the entrance door, which is a contrast of wooden brown and black and white geometric designs to the sofa pillows and wall pieces, the overarching theme of the space, remains consistent. The clarity with which space was conceptualised and designed, is the perfect example of the palpable vision of the client for minimalism and virtuosity of the studio in bringing that vision to life.

Modern Shack
A mix of English and contemporary design with sleek furniture style.

This is a masterpiece one can count on to experience unceasing nobility in the form of residential design. The monochromatic abstract scrap design of the walls along with the lighted wall centrepiece in the hall is perfect for a contemporary feel with an English flavour. And, the dull ocean blue coloured sofas and the unique wooden furniture add to it. The bedroom is designed to make one feel the enchantment of utopia with its light brown basic, minimally curated walls and arrangement of the bed, curtains and ceiling in the colour of blissful white. The shining, slightly yellowish minimalist cupboard contemporary and cutting edge look. The sleek design of the furniture contrasted with the various well-crafted elements of design gives the space a texture of modernistic vogue along with basal simplicity. The sheer placement of the furniture and attention to positioning itself makes the space one of a kind, “a modern shack!”

The Cottage House
Warm, earthly colours to experience the leisure of cottage style.

We’ve all experienced the rainy earth smell, the joy of looking at a flower, and the comforting feeling of a hug from a loved one. Now, imagine a mix of all of this translated into an interior design space. Welcome to the cottage house! As soon as one enters the hall, the huge, visually pleasing, colourful and welcoming carpet beneath the glass table catches attention. The colour of the sofas is earthly enough to give an embracing feeling and is lightly toned to give it a comforting yet prosperous look. The mirror wall piece with knitted golden borders is probably the centre of attraction for anyone who enters the space. And, it is well contrasted with the rectangular engravement of the white walls and the beautiful blue vases loaded with flowers on a basic yet intricately designed wooden table. The wall piece over the dining table is one of a kind and reflects the unique feel and flavour the design intended to provide. Starting from the dining area there’s so much the eyes can devour. The velvet blue chairs of the dining area, the design of the kitchen nearby, the lush green plant in a golden vase, the bright yellow lights hanging off a distance from the ceiling and the colour contrast of brown and white shining in the kitchen; everything is well crafted and designed to mot just experience the royalty and comfort but to make incredible memories!
