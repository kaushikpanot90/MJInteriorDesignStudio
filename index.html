About us

MJ interior design studio is a full-service interior design firm based in Ahmedabad. We provide custom interior design services and creative solutions for residential as well as commercial projects.

The studio was founded in 2020, by Maulik Jepiwala, who graduated from the prestige CEPT university with a degree in interior design. Passionate about his craft, he has built his professional career in interior design from scratch, from just being a recent graduate to owning the experience of various unique residential projects in his credit, which later evolved to demonstrating excellence in various medium & large scale projects.
